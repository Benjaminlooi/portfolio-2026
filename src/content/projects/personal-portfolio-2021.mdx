## Overview

My first portfolio, built in 2021 with React and Tailwind CSS. It served me well for four years — through job applications, freelance pitches, and client meetings.

Eventually, the tech and design started feeling dated. I&apos;d outgrown the template-style layout, and the lack of a blog or proper SEO meant I was leaving opportunities on the table. That frustration is what led me to rebuild everything from scratch as the [2026 version](/projects/portfolio-2026) you&apos;re currently viewing.

I&apos;m keeping this here as a marker of how far I&apos;ve come — both in design sensibility and technical depth.
